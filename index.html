DiscorJ

A DJ bot for Discord

Add to Discord

"@DiscorJ, play Ed Sheeran."

DiscorJ is a DJ bot for your Discord server. It does one thing and is really good at what it does.

No commands.

All you have to do to use DiscorJ is mention it and type what you want it to do. DiscorJ will take care of the rest.

Add to Discord

@DiscorJ, can you play We Are Number One?

Sure. Playing We Are Number One | LazyTown...

@DiscorJ, no! Wait! I hate that song!

Okay, skipping We Are Number One.

Advanced features.

DiscorJ has an advanced queue system, with operations such as play next, play last, and more.

Add to Discord

@DiscorJ, play Million Reasons next.

You got it. "Million Reasons - Lady Gaga" will play next.

@DiscorJ, add Never Gonna Give You Up to the queue.

Okay, "Never Gonna Give You Up" has been added to the queue.
